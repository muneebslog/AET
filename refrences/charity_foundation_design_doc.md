# Charity Foundation Website Design Document

## 1. Design Philosophy

This website must feel **emotionally powerful, credible, and modern**.

Not NGO-generic.
Not corporate.
Not template-looking.

It should feel like:
- A global movement
- A faith-driven mission (if applicable)
- A transparent, structured organization
- A premium digital presence

The tone is **dignified, warm, confident, and trustworthy**.

---

## 2. Visual Identity

### Primary Emotion
Compassion + Urgency + Hope

### Color System

**Primary Color:** Deep Burgundy / Rich Crimson  
Used for hero background, CTAs, donation highlights.

**Accent Color:** Warm Gold or Soft Peach  
Used for highlights, badges, subtle glow elements.

**Base Background:** Soft Off-White / Light Beige  
Avoid pure white to reduce harsh contrast.

**Dark Sections:** Deep Maroon gradient overlays  
Used to create emotional intensity.

### Typography

- Headings: Bold, modern sans-serif with strong presence.
- Body: Clean, highly readable sans-serif.
- Large hero headings with generous letter spacing.

Text hierarchy must feel intentional and premium.

---

## 3. Layout System

### Spacing Philosophy

- Generous vertical spacing
- Wide containers
- Large hero section (full-height feel)
- Breathing room between sections

Avoid clutter.

---

## 4. Homepage Structure

### 4.1 Navigation Bar

- Transparent over hero
- Logo on left
- Menu centered or right-aligned
- Primary "Donate Now" button highlighted

Navigation links:
- Home
- Current Appeals
- Our Programs
- Get Involved
- News & Events
- Donate Now (Primary CTA)

---

### 4.2 Hero Section

Full-width emotional image.

Image should:
- Be real (not stock-feeling)
- Show dignity, not pity
- Feature direct human eye contact

Overlay:
- Deep burgundy gradient
- Subtle abstract pattern or shape in background

Content:
- Large emotional headline
- Short mission paragraph
- Primary Donate button
- Secondary Learn More button

Hero must feel bold and global.

---

### 4.3 Featured Appeals Section

Card-based layout.

Each card includes:
- Real image
- Campaign title
- Short description
- Progress bar
- Donation tiers
- Donate button

Cards should have:
- Soft rounded corners
- Subtle shadow
- Hover lift effect

---

### 4.4 Impact Statistics Section

Minimal, centered layout.

Large numbers with small descriptive text.

Example:
- 1,250 Families Supported
- 300 Water Projects Completed
- 5 Countries Served

Background can alternate slightly darker tone for contrast.

---

### 4.5 Share Barakah / Active Causes

Grid of image-based cause tiles.

Each tile:
- Photo
- Cause title
- Short description
- Donate button

Cards should feel consistent and structured.

---

### 4.6 Faith / Mission Statement Section

Large headline:
"One Donation, Infinite Rewards"

This section should feel reflective and spiritual.

Use softer background tone.

Include:
- Short explanatory paragraph
- Subtle divider

---

### 4.7 News & Updates Section

Three-column layout.

Each article:
- Image
- Title
- Small metadata
- Read More button

Clean and editorial.

---

### 4.8 Global Reach Section

Dark, high-contrast section.

Include:
- Headline
- Short description
- Partner logos
- CTA button

This section reinforces credibility.

---

## 5. Design Language

### Imagery

- Authentic photography
- Natural lighting
- Human-centered framing
- Avoid over-posed stock images

### UI Style

- Rounded corners (medium radius)
- Soft shadows
- Subtle hover animations
- Smooth transitions

### Buttons

Primary:
- Solid burgundy background
- White text
- Slight shadow

Secondary:
- Outline style
- Transparent background

Hover states must feel intentional and smooth.

---

## 6. Trust Architecture

To avoid looking "vibe-coded", the website must include:

- Real data numbers
- Transparent financial breakdowns
- Real testimonials
- Clear contact information
- Legal registration details
- SSL and secure donation messaging

Trust is visual + structural.

---

## 7. Tone of Content

Avoid vague statements like:
"We help people in need."

Instead use:
"In 2025 alone, we delivered food aid to 1,247 families across 3 regions."

Specificity builds authority.

---

## 8. Interaction & Motion

Subtle animation only:
- Fade-in on scroll
- Smooth progress bar animation
- Button hover transitions

No excessive motion.

Premium, calm, confident.

---

## 9. Overall Feeling Summary

The website must feel:
- Global
- Organized
- Mission-driven
- Emotionally strong
- Technically polished
